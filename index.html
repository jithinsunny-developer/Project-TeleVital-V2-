TeleVital

Enhancing the quality of contactless health assessment.

This platform is designed to bring a significant change in the telemedicine industry. TeleVital helps you in scheduling an appointment with a doctor, make video consultation, taking vital tests through contactless methods, analysing chest X-Ray for Covid-19 and a lot more.

Some of the best features of our application!

Hassle-free appointment scheduling

TeleVital brings to you a new trend of hassle-free appointment scheduling and tele-consulting through our user friendly platform.

Video Consultation & Vitals Monitoring

Helping patients connect with their doctors through remote video consultation in realtime and helping the healthcare professionals monitor the vitals of a patient remotely and through cotactless methods.

Customised Dashboard

We believe in providing the best user experience for our patients and healthcare professionals. We have customised dashboard for the doctors and patients to keep a track of their appointments, schedules, prescription and a lot more.

AI X-Ray Analyser & Voice based Prescription

Cost and time effective solution for quicker analysis of Covid-19 through AI based chest X-Ray analyser. The new style of prescription writing through voice based contactless prescription writing.
